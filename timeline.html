Journey through Time

Key milestones in the history of space exploration, marking humanity's progressive steps into the universe.

1957

Sputnik 1

The Soviet Union launched the first artificial Earth satellite, initiating the Space Age and the Space Race.

1961

First Human in Space

Yuri Gagarin orbits the Earth aboard Vostok 1, becoming the first human to travel into outer space.

1969

Apollo 11 Moon Landing

Neil Armstrong and Buzz Aldrin become the first humans to walk on the lunar surface.

1977

Voyager Probes Launched

Voyager 1 and 2 are launched to study the outer Solar System. They are now in interstellar space.

1990

Hubble Space Telescope

Deployed into low Earth orbit, Hubble has provided some of the most detailed images of the cosmos.

1998

International Space Station

The first modular component of the ISS was launched, establishing a permanent human presence in space.

2012

Curiosity Rover on Mars

NASA's rover successfully lands in Gale Crater, searching for signs of past microbial life.

2021

James Webb Space Telescope

The most powerful telescope ever built is launched to observe the first galaxies formed after the Big Bang.
